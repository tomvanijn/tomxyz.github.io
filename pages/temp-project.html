BEAR'S DEN

2025 Bear's Den Bunker

Created for the upcoming title Broken. This cold war era underground bunker located in the heart of the middle east. Currently retrofitted to serve as a base of operations for a bioterrorist group. Created as a visual prototype.

Responsible for all aspects except the vehicle.

Scene rendered in UE4

Tools: Maya, Photoshop, Unreal 4, Ndo2, Ddo, Substance Designer
